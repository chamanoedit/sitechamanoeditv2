⚠️ CRITICAL — MULTI-SCENE EDIT. 5 SCENES:

SCENE 1 (0–3 sec): Golden hour. Sweeping aerial over a hillside COVERED with thousands of people — a sea of faces, murmuring, hungry. The modern man stands among them, looking around at the impossible crowd. A small tug on his sleeve: the BOY, holding up his little basket — 5 loaves, 2 fish — big hopeful eyes.

SCENE 2 (3–6 sec): The man kneels to the boy's level, looks at the tiny basket, then at the thousands of people — a soft disbelieving smile: "this won't be enough… but okay." He takes the boy's hand and they weave through the crowd together, camera tracking them, until they reach JESUS — and the man places the basket in His hands.

SCENE 3 (6–9 sec): CLOSE-UP — Jesus lifts the bread, gives thanks with closed eyes, and BREAKS it. A burst of golden light-crumbs. He breaks it again — the basket GLOWS and refills from within, bread rising like it's breathing. Slow push-in, god-rays intensifying. The boy's jaw drops. The man's eyes go wide.

SCENE 4 (9–12 sec): THE WAVE — dynamic camera flying low over the crowd as baskets multiply hand to hand, an expanding ripple of golden light and passing bread spreading across the entire hillside, children laughing, families eating, light particles everywhere. The man is in the middle of it, passing baskets, laughing, overwhelmed.

SCENE 5 (12–15 sec): The feast settles into golden calm. TWELVE baskets overflowing in the foreground. The boy sits beside the man, both eating bread, crumbs on their faces. The man looks at the half-eaten loaf in his hand, then toward Jesus in the distance — who is already looking back at him with a warm smile. Final close: the man's face — moved, full, a quiet laugh.
